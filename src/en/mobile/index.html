What is PlatON

  • Computing Factory

    • Connect multi-source data and heterogeneousnetwork infrastructure

    • Integrate resources of computing power, data and algorithms globally

    • Facilitate the exchange of data and computing power

  • Service Marketplace

    • Resources and applications offered as services

    • Each node can release and request services

    • Built-in basic services to deliver a full range of computing, storage and communication capabilities

  • Super Clearing House

    • Provide operational-level support in value exchange of services and applications

    • Services covers billing, payment, clearing, settlement and dispute resolution

    • Data as assets and data exchange as tokens

  • Privacy Protector

    • Protect data privacy with Secure Multi-Party Computation (MPC)

    • Hide identities and details of a transaction using Zero-Knowledge Proofs (ZKP)

    • Create a fully encrypted network using Homomorphic Encryption

Isomorphic Multi-chain Architecture

RELOAD Overlay Network /Master Chain + multiple Application Chains

  • Decentralized and Distributed RPC Framework

    • P2P service discovery and service governance

    • Blockchain as a Service that sets up application chains to support different business scenarios

    • Network-wide service provided from any node; Services can be published through any node. They can be discovered and called by the entire network

  • Unified User Account

    • Access to the whole network through one unified account

    • Manage Energons of different blockchains through the unified account

    • Use services from different blockchains through the unified account

  • Basic Resources Sharing

    • Basic services such as computing, storage, data, routing and messaging are independent of blockchains

    • Application chains can share these basic services using adaptive resource allocation

  • Multi-Chain Transparent Routing

    • Blockchain’s location transparency eliminates the need for a user to locate the nodes of the target application chain

    • A user can launch a transaction from any node to any blockchain on the network

  • Cross-chain Energon transfer

    • Each application chain can issue its own Energon

    • A low-cost decentralized exchange is available to enable the conversion and transfer between master chain ATP, application chain Energon and external assets

Meta Computing Framework - Monad

Decouple computation from consensus

  • Secure and Efficient Blockchain Computation

    • Cryptography-based off-chain computation supports large-scale and complicated computations

    • Circuit-based parallel computing enables linear scalability of computing power

    • Computing channels based on verifiable computation and timelocks serve as the “Clearing House” and “Arbitral Tribunal” for computing power transactions

  • Meta Smart Contract - Sophia

    • Integrate on-chain and off-chain multi-source heterogeneous data

    • Support Java and other general programming languages

    • Programs compiled into circuits to support hardware acceleration

  • Data Privacy Protection

    • Use Homomorphic Encryption and Zero-Knowledge Proof to protect the privacy of on-chain data as well as off-chain single-source data

    • Use MPC to safeguard the privacy of off-chain multi-source data

  • Proof-of-Verifiable-Computation Concensus - Giskard

    • Block producers are elected based on the value of computing contribution weighted stake

    • Use an optimized PBFT mechanism to generate blocks and reach consensus

    • Cryptography-based state transition proof provides a quick way to verify the correctness of transactions while avoiding repetitive computation

  • Computing Specific Hardware

    • meta smart contracts which are compiled into circuits are a natural fit

    • Enhance computation efficiency while reducing power consumption and cost

    • Co-evolution of software and hardware

Application and Ecology

Data Transaction

PlatON provides computation-based public infrastructure for data transactions around the world, where secure data exchange application chains and applications can be established to provide institutions and individuals with safe and reliable data exchange and collaborative computing services. These help to protect data privacy during data sharing and gain economic benefits without losing ownership. PlatON assists data owners and users in generating reasonable ratings and pricing in data transactions.

Global Scientific Computation

PlatON allows computation tasks to be split into sub-tasks and assigned to computing resources around the world. PlatON can encourage and integrate scattered computing resources to contribute to the massive-computing-resource-consumption computations such as scientific computation. The proper utilization of computing power allows scientific research institutions and commercial groups to carry out cheaper and more efficient scientific computation.

Authentication

A decentralized identity management system based on PlatON can solve problems of traditional centralized systems, such as single point of failure, the malicious collection and abuse of user’s private data. A complete user portrait profile can be pieced together through collaborative computing which is characteristic of privacy protection. Agencies will be able to identify theirusers more effectively, and therefore serve them better. The strengthening and transmission of trust is realized by keeping records on blockchain every time an identity is certified to create a chain of evidence that cannot be tampered with.

Medical health

The medical heath and genetic data of individuals have the highest confidential requirements and the largest amount of all types of data. Based on the PlatON, an open service platform for medical information can be built，which shares unified and universal data exchange standards, to realize the interconnection and interoperability of heterogeneous data among different organizations, thereby promoting the further analysis and use of medical and genetic data. Applications such as the global gene banks, personal life-cycle health accounts and medical data clusters can then be implemented.

Credit Investigation System

Traditional credit investigation systems are now facing the classic "blind men and an elephant" problem due to limited sources of third-party data and the constraints of data sharing. A distributed social credit system based on PlatON with privacy protection will be able to integrate data from multiple sources, develop credit models for different scenarios, allow individuals to manage their own credit data, and allows organizations to engage in joint credit investigation. The cost of credit investigation will be reduced for the society as a whole and a more sound social credit system will be built.

Social Network

A next-generation social network can be built on the PlatON to support peer-to-peer communication and data exchange across regions and across applications. The network can operate independently of centralized institutions. The advantages include decentralization, privacy protection, security and incentive compatibility. At the same time, users control all the data they generate. PlatON is a natural carrier for depository certification and copyright registration. Users can therefore build up their personal credit, brand, and influence in the digital world.

IoT and Industrial Internet

PlatON can equip IoT with infrastructure where data security and privacy is guaranteed, edge computing capability readily available, and a computing power sharing platform accessible. This will accelerate the development of IoT applications in fields such as smart manufacturing, Internet-of-Vehicles, agriculture, smart cities, and distributed energy management. Also innovations in business models will be promoted.
